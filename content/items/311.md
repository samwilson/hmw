## Transcription

SECRET

(To be destroyed in the event of a forced landing in enemy or neutral territory.)

Aircraft No FH. 302 Captain Sgt WILSON

Commitment 459 Squadron

Destination M.E.

Remarks —

Certified that the above mentioned pilot has been instructed to proceed with
this aircraft to the destination indicated.

These orders are to be adhered to unless direct instructions to the
contrary are received from Air Ministry, or some Competent Authority
acting on behalf of the Air Ministry.

Date 31 MAY 1942

{?|R R Colis} S/L\
Officer Commanding,\
1444 Ferry Training Flight,\
{u|R.A.F., Horsham St. Faith.}

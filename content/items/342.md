## Transcription

AUS 406376\
P/O. W.M. WILSON\
c/o RAAF BASE POST OFFICE\
MIDDLE EAST

98 Herbert Rd\
Shenton Park\
Sept 9th 1943

No. 5

My dear Murray

Yesterday the 3 girls came
to dinner, & Helen & John Solly were
both here also. But I missed all of
my own absent loved ones very much. It was not
at all the same, under these {?} conditions.
The phone was ringing constantly with good wishes,
a telegram from Aunt Ray, & one from Joan. Letters
from Gordon, Eva, & Charlie, earlier in the week, and
yesterday morning one from Rae, right on the day.
The girls brought beautiful flowers, & Joan sent a
bouquet, daffodils & smoke bush. {?} Margaret, who
insisted on making the sweets for dinner, trifle &
a baked rhubarb with custard & meringue, very nice.
She gave me a book, 'Behold new Holland', and Jean
brought 2 very nice twels, & a block of choc, & a small

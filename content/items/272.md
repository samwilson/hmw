## Transcription

To HAH 12/2/1947

From Marjorie (? Maime){br}
During illness staying at Edith's

Mr Hall{br}
Woopenatty{br}
Arrino{br}
WA

98 Herbert Rd{br}
Shenton Park{br}
12.2.47

Dear Mr Hall

Thank you very
very much for the chocolates,
which Judith bought to me it
was very sweet of you to send
them to me.

The family bring Alan down
every week-end and he is
a darling baby. Last Sunday
they took some snaps I hope
they will turn out well.

With kind regards

Marjorie

Office of Origin: Hay St West Perth

No of Words: 25

Time of lodgement: 1 PM

Aubrey Hall{br}
Woopenatty{br}
Arrino

Already inquired and written you regarding
archdeacon his condition statisfactory is
comfortable and having visitors hospital
advises anxiety unncessary

Love{br}
Margaret

Murray was at No. 3 School of General Reconnaissance Squires Gate from 29 September to 29 November 1941.

## Transcription

Serial No. 33/35GR.

Royal Air Force.

Air Navigator's Certificate.

This is to Certify that Sergeant. W.M. Wilson

has successfully qualified as an Air Navigator Second class.

Dated 22/12/41

A W Rogers

Group Captain

Officer Commanding School of General Reconnaissance.

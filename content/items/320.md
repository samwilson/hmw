## Transcription

Hotel Cottesloe \
Cottesloe \
21/2/46

Mr A Hall. \
Arrino

Dear Aubrey

I received your letter on Monday
and was glad to hear that things are
going along O.K. at Woopenatty.

I am sending up a cook
by Saturdays train. His name is
Harry Whitehead. I think I
left a new mattress in the room if
I did not ask Mr Smith to get one
out of the spare room.

I sent 1 pair of trousers to
George they were the only kind they
had, if he wants another pair like
them let me know and I will send
another pair.

I shall be back on Friday
week.

Your sincerely\
R.A. Weill

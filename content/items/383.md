## Transcription

Woopenatty\
Arrino\
30/11/48

Dear Aubrey,

I received your welcome letter
of a few months ago, and have been
intending to drop you a line. My
correspondence is mostly anything from
twelve months to twelve years behind.

Glad that you had a good
trip to Perth, and that you were
having an enjoyable time meeting
old friends. How do you like
Perth {?}

Well Woopenatty is still on
the map. Jack Fred and Gerry are
still here and send you their kind
regards.

We managed to get the shearing
finished on the last Tiesday in
September, and I did not {?} any
{?} at {?} the end of it. It was
a very long drawn out job, and the
blowflys were very bad, which made
a {?} of work owing to the slow rate
of shearing.

## Transcription

AUS 406376\
PILOT OFFICER W.M. WILSON.\
75 OPERATIONAL TRAINING UNIT\
R.A.F.

MIDDLE EAST

23 STANLEY ST.\
NEDLANDS W.A.

5th AUGUST 1943

My Dear Murray,

My first Airgraph! Jean wrote you
yesterday saying the Americans have taken over the
SRRB for their Red Cross and entertainments.
They are spending about £2000 and should look
pretty good, he has written in detail. We are
all well. Adele has gone into a new class at
school, and is very chilled with herself.

Mother, Margaret & Jean & perhaps Mrs Polley
are coming here for lunch tomorrow. Mother
seems to be very please with her tenants,
and it is certainly a weight off our minds
knowing she is not alone. My fowls are
doing very well, getting an average of 9 or 10 eggs
a day, not bad. Might give mother 3 fowls for her
birthday, there's not much else to get. Love,
Adele.
(MRS) ADELE LANG ''A. Lang''

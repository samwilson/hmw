The front is a photographic print, with the reverse being a photo copy of the original postcard, taped to the front.
The front has the inscription (not scanned) of "Ernest Albert Donegan C 19". The postcard text reads:

{{{quotation
Thursday

Just a card — do you recognise them?
Recd paper OK today tks D. very much — your letter was real good D, but what was the matter on the phone last night
— you were very naughty! Hope you are all well Best love to Both — B.

Miss L. Innes

Chidlow's Well.
}}}

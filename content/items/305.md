INSTRUCTIONS

(See A.F.O. 10/J/1.)

—

1. This book is an official document and is the property of His Majesty's Government.
2. An accurate and detailed record is to be kept in the log of all flights undertaken in service aircraft by the individual to whom it relates.
3. The annual summary and assessment will be completed on Form A. 18A and inserted in the appropriate page of the log. This form will also be used when a pilot is posted or attached to another unit for flying duties.

## Transcription

(Mundabullangana)

Munda Station \
Pt Hedland \
17/5/40

To Mr E. Hall Cossack

Dear Erney

I am trying to
get as much information
as possible regarding the
earliest pioneers of the North
West, and I will be much
obliged to you if you will
find time to drop me a
line giving what you
know of {?|earliest} day
pioneers / Such as first
{?|publicans} in Cossack &

{{{page-break}}}

2/

Roebourne, first pearlers,
first pastoralists etc.
I am not sure how old
you are, but I was
born in 1864 & came
to Cossack on 17th April 1879
two days before Billy Cook.
You will know more about
this matter than any
man living today.
I trust you will be good
enough to let me know
Trusting this finds
you O.K. & wishing you
the best.

I am yours sincerely \
R.L. Mackay

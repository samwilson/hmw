## Transcription

AUS 406376\
PILOT OFFICER W.M. WILSON\
75 OPERATIONAL TRAINING UNIT\
R.A.F.\
MIDDLE EAST.

PERTH\
WA

MRS R.G. AGNEW\
37 MYERS ST.\
NEDLANDS\
W.AUST.

July 22nd. 1943.

My dear Murray,

This is the first airgraph
letter I have sent you, but there will be
many more to follow.

I got your cable & was
thrilled. You must have got a bunch of
mail at that time.

I am very sad at the
moment. Bob has been transferred to
headquarters, Melbourne, and he has
gone. I am thinking of taking a job.
I know I am only one of thousands
to lose my husband but that does
not make it any easier for me.

Garrick is very well & is
getting a big boy now. He is nearly
as tall as I am & weighs about 8st. 7lbs.

I was at Adele's for lunch
yesterday. They are all well. We are very
glad about mother's new arrangement
they are a nice couple.

Much love from us all\
from Jean.

*Jean Agew.*

DOROTHY JEAN AGNEW

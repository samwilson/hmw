## Transcription

AUS406376\
P/O. W.M. WILSON\
75 OPERATIONAL TRAINING UNIT.\
R.A.F.\
MIDDLE EAST.

Mrs. J. H. Wilson\
Senders Address: 98 Herbert Rd\
Shenton	 Park.\
July 6th 1943.

My dear Murray.

Your cable recd 5th. delighted
that you had such good mail. Hope you are well
Am letting part of the house, to an air pilot
& his wife, friends of Gwyneth Chambers.
Glad you are able to know so soon, as I am
sure you will be more satisfied that I
have company. He will be away for a
week, then at home for a few days.
& is willing to do anything you me & is fond
of gardening.
Auntie Eva is staying with
me for a week, then later will return
to the country. Families are all well.
Mr Jolly came to see me last week.
His wife comes by train from Adelaide
on July 15th. Take care of yourself.
all my love darling.
Yours ever
Mother

EDITH OLIVE WILSON\
Edith Olive Wilson.\
EO Wilson.

**Laetitia Hall** was born in 1822, and baptized on the same day as two of her siblings,
on 6 May 1827 at St Mary's church in Lambeth.{ref|hmwtree}

Married Robert on 5 May 1847.{ref|hmwtree}

She had twins Robert Edward and Frederick Henry Valentine in 1854.
In January of that year she was travelling and was taken ill at "the 47 mile tree on the Albany Road",
and stayed there for some weeks until she gave birth on February 18.
Afterwards, her brother Anderton took her and the infants
by bullock cart to the family's farm at {link|tags/wongong|Wongong}.{ref|Hall1854}

Laetitia died in 1877 and is buried at East Perth Cemeteries in the Hall grave.{ref|hmwtree}{ref|grave}

**William Shakespeare Hall jnr.** was the youngest child of Shakespeare and Hannah Hall.
He died aged only four months, in 1879.

William was born on 8 or 9 September 1878 in Cossack.{ref|birth}
The family notice in the Perth newspaper *The Inquirer and Commercial News* of 16 October gives his birth date as the 9th,{ref|birth}
and the family gravestone at Cossack says the 8th.{ref|grave}
It is presumed that the former source has the correct date.

He died on 19 January 1879.{ref|grave}

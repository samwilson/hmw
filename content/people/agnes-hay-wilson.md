'''Agnes May Wilson''' was a telegraphist{ref|electoral} in Victoria, Australia.
She was born in Brunswich on 17 August 1878,{ref|1}{ref|birthIndex} and in 1905 was living with her family on Chaucer Crescent in Canterbury.
She never married, and died in 1958 aged 80 (cerebral thrombosis), in Sydney.{ref|1}{ref|deathReg}
Some sources have her middle name as 'Hay' but this appears to be a typo.

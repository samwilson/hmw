**Flora Louise Sherlock** (known as **Louise**{ref|letter1968}) was born in Perth, Western Australia, in 1891.
Her parents were Francis Hilton Sherlock and Mary Sherlock née Huddart.{ref|birth}

*The West Australian*, 21 December 1916:{ref|marriage}

{{{quotation
HALL—SHERLOCK.—On December 14, at St. Mary's Church, Colin-street, West Perth,
by the Rev. Thos. Whitehead, Herbert H. Hall, A.I.F.,
eldest son of Mrs. F. W. Teesdale, of St. Leonard's-avenue, West Leederville, to Flora Louise,
youngest daughter of Mr. and Mrs. F. H. Sherlock, of Victoria Park.
}}}

Herbert died in 1918, and at the end of 1919 Louise and her mother and sister moved from Perth to Sydney.
She was living in Kitchener Road in Artarmon in 1968.{ref|letter1968}

Flora died on 18 January 1981, and her ashes are interred at Macquarie Park Cemetery in Sydney.{ref|deathIndex}{ref|findagrave}

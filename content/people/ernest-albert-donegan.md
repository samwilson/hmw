**Ernest "Bert"**{ref|item396} **Albert Donegan** was born in Toodyay, Western Australia on 26 May 1887.{ref|EADoneganGrave}{ref|BMDBirth}
He was the son of Thomas Donegan and Charlotte Donegan née Herbert.
Married Lynda Innes on Tuesday, October 10, 1911, at Chidlow's Well.{ref|marriage}
With her had first {link|people/peter-innes-donegan|Peter} in June 1913,
followed by Pat in 1916,
and Judy in 1918.
Died on 23 December 1956 and was buried at Karrakatta (Anglican BA 598).{ref|EADoneganGrave}
